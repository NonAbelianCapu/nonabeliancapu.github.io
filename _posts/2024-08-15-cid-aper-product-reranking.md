This project was developed in collaboration with **CID** (Interinstitutional Center for Data Science) and **APER**.

The main objective was to design a product **re-ranking service** for online stores, improving relevance and ordering quality on top of existing ranking pipelines.

### Methods & Tools
- NLP-driven relevance features
- Traditional ranking strategies combined with machine-learning signals
- Evaluation of ranking quality and ordering precision
