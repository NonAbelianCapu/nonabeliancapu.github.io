This project for **Banco de la Provincia de Buenos Aires (BAPRO) State Bank** focused on building an **AI-based weekly indicator of economic activity** for Buenos Aires Province.

The workflow integrated high-frequency signals from banking data with official economic data to produce robust short-horizon measurements.

### Methods & Tools
- Mixed-source economic data integration
- Machine-learning nowcasting workflow
- Weekly indicator estimation and validation
